Our History

Rooted in Tradition, Grown with Passion

Palmera Luxe began with a simple idea — to honor the sacred fruit of the desert by elevating it into an unforgettable gourmet experience. Founded in Morocco and born from generations of date farmers in the Saharan heartlands, our journey is one of authenticity, respect, and refinement.

What started as a family craft in Moroccan date cultivation has blossomed into a brand that blends heritage with innovation. Each dates we offer is hand-selected at its peak, filled and garnished with care using only premium ingredients like roasted nuts, citrus zest, Belgian chocolate, or fragrant spices.

We believe that gifting or enjoying a date should be a ritual — elegant, emotional, and memorable. Palmera Luxe is more than a product; it’s a taste of Moroccan tradition, wrapped in modern luxury.

“Our dates carry the story of our ancestors — now beautifully retold in every bite.”

Our Premium Dates

Mejhoul

Known as the "King of Dates", Medjool dates are large, soft, and incredibly sweet. Grown in the Tafilalt region, they're Morocco’s most luxurious variety.

Sukkary

Sukkary dates, originating from the Al Qassim region of Saudi Arabia, are often referred to as 'royal dates' due to their exceptional flavor.

Safawi

Safawi dates are another date variety grown in Saudi Arabia in Madinah. They are dark black, larger than usual, and similar to medjool dates.

 Naturally Noble   Naturally Noble 

Boufeggous

A variety of Moroccan dates, known for their thick and soft texture, fragrant flavor, and rich nutritional value.

Jihel

Jihel is a traditional Moroccan date variety known for its chewy texture, rich earthy flavor, and long shelf life.

Ajwa

Ajwa is a prized variety of dates originally from Medina, known for its soft texture, mildly sweet taste, and religious significance in Islamic tradition

Benefits of Moroccan Dates

Controls Blood Pressure

Rich in potassium and magnesium, dates help balance fluids and lower blood pressure naturally.

Boosts Energy

Dates contain natural sugars like glucose and sucrose which provide quick energy and help regulate blood sugar. Perfect for athletes.

Improves Vision

With high vitamin A content, dates improve night vision and protect against eye disorders.

Fights Insomnia

Contains tryptophan, which promotes melatonin and serotonin—hormones that regulate healthy sleep.

Enhances Digestion

High in fiber, dates ease digestion and help with constipation by improving bowel movement.

Strengthens Nervous System

Loaded with potassium and B vitamins, dates boost brain function and protect the nervous system.
